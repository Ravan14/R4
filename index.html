The Problem We Solve

Current Earth Observation systems are fragmented. Optical struggles at night/under cloud; SAR lacks spectral detail; AIS/ADS-B/APRS miss non-cooperative targets — creating gaps in awareness and delaying response.

Our Solutions

Synchronous fusion of hardware and software: the Insight Set satellite cluster and the ℝ Analytics Platform work together to deliver defence-grade intelligence in a single pass.

The Hardware: “Insight Set” Constellation

Synchronous Fusion

The "Insight Set" is a tightly controlled cluster of three satellites — one Microsat and two CubeSats — flying in formation to capture Electro-Optical, Thermal Infrared, SAR and RF data over the same Area of Interest at the same instant.

  • Clustered formation flight keeps all three spacecraft synchronized in time and geometry, enabling alignment across modalities.
  • Zero-latency fusion: confirm Activity, Material and Location in a single pass instead of waiting for multiple overflights or external cues.

The Software: ℝ Analytics Platform

Proprietary Analytics Platform

The ℝ Analytics Platform ingests raw multi-modal telemetry from the Insight Set and transforms it into mission-ready intelligence products that plug directly into C2 and GIS workflows.

  • ℝ Analytics Platform: automates sensor fusion, pattern learning, anomaly scoring and data processing so analysts see curated intelligence instead of raw pixels.
  • Constellation Control: centralized tasking and cross-cueing for adaptive response, allowing the cluster to re-task itself as new activity is detected.

FAQs

How do you handle cloud cover and night operations?

We combine SAR (cloud-penetrating) with optical and hyperspectral sensors, then fuse them through the ℝ Insight Engine. This ensures consistent intelligence regardless of weather, time of day, or visibility conditions.

What data formats and products do you deliver?

Our outputs include orthorectified imagery, spectral indices, object detection layers, vector alerts, temporal change maps, and standardized GIS layers. Delivery is via OGC-compliant services, REST APIs, or secure data packages tailored to client infrastructure.

Can your products integrate with existing C2/GIS tools?

Yes. We provide standards-based endpoints and SDK snippets for integration into common C2, ISR, and GIS platforms. Multi-tenant access controls ensure secure role-based integration.

What industries or sectors can benefit from your technology?

Defence and intelligence agencies, disaster management authorities, environmental agencies, agricultural organisations, maritime and aviation regulators, urban planners, and humanitarian NGOs all benefit from our multi-domain solutions.

How secure is the data you provide?

Security is paramount. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with access controlled through multi-factor authentication and detailed audit logs. We comply with defence and critical-infrastructure security standards.

What is the revisit rate of your constellation?

Our Insight Set constellation is designed to achieve a global average revisit of 120 minutes, with regional tasking enabling much higher refresh rates for priority zones.

How do you ensure data accuracy and reliability?

We employ physics-informed models, calibration with ground truth, and redundancy across sensing modalities. Automated quality checks and AI-driven anomaly detection maintain high reliability.

Do you offer customised solutions?

Absolutely. We co-design mission-specific data pipelines and analytic dashboards with customers, ensuring outputs are tailored to operational needs.

What support do you provide for disaster response?

We maintain a dedicated rapid-response tasking protocol for natural disasters, with high-priority acquisition, expedited processing, and direct collaboration with relief agencies to deliver critical intelligence in hours.

Can commercial organisations also access your services?

Yes. Beyond defence and government, we serve commercial sectors including insurance, logistics, energy, agriculture, and infrastructure management, providing dual-use value across industries.
